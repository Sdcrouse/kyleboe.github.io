Contract Work Aplenty
Mar 28, 2015, 5:40 PM PDT
The past couple days have been pretty crazy. I have been looking for work for about 2 months now with no luck. This last week I had been offered some contract work for a company in Vancouver. They sent the paperwork over to me a few days ago and I got it finalized and back to them on Wednesday. The moment I sent the response email with the finalized paperwork, I recieved a facebook message with a job offer. The friend's dad who offered me the job asked me to come in as soon as I could for a small interview/introduction. I immediately went to their office for said interview/introduction and while I was there, I recieved a message about more potential contract work. It left me pretty speechless after looking for work so long and then suddenly having the opportunity to work three jobs. I ended up having to turn down one of the jobs simply because of there being only so many hours in a day. I am so excited to finally be back working.
